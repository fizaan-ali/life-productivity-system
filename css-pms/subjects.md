# CSS/PMS — Subjects

## Compulsory Subjects

### English Essay
- **Weight**: 100 marks
- **Key Topics**: Current issues, social topics, governance, international affairs
- **Study Strategy**: Practice essay writing weekly, review model essays
- **Overlaps With**: Political Science (International Relations, Governance)

### English Precis & Composition
- **Weight**: 100 marks
- **Key Topics**: Precis writing, comprehension, grammar, vocabulary
- **Study Strategy**: Daily precis practice, vocabulary building
- **Overlaps With**: —

### Current Affairs
- **Weight**: 100 marks
- **Key Topics**: Pakistan current affairs, international current affairs, economy, governance
- **Study Strategy**: Daily newspaper reading, monthly current affairs digest
- **Overlaps With**: BS Political Science (International Relations)

### Pakistan Affairs
- **Weight**: 100 marks
- **Key Topics**: History, politics, economy, culture, foreign policy
- **Study Strategy**: Textbook study, historical timeline review
- **Overlaps With**: BS Political Science (Pakistan Studies)

### Islamic Studies / Comparative Religion
- **Weight**: 100 marks
- **Key Topics**: Islamic principles, history, comparative religion
- **Study Strategy**: Textbook review, topic-wise notes
- **Overlaps With**: —

### General Science & Ability
- **Weight**: 100 marks
- **Key Topics**: Everyday science, math, logic, analytical reasoning
- **Study Strategy**: Practice quantitative reasoning, review science basics
- **Overlaps With**: BS Computer Science (logical reasoning)
